Note: As this method connects to a lakehouse, it can be scheduled to refersh on your desired schedule.

Notebook Instructions:
1) upload the nb_ModelCatalog notebook to your desired workspace.  (Note, it can be renamed, if desired.)
   Note, the notebook must be in the same workspace as your lakehouse.
2) Fill in the required options in the first cell (workspace name, lakehouse name, etc.)
3) Enter 'Import' as the option for model_type
4) Run the notebook to create and populate the lakehouse. (If the lakehouse already exists, only tables will be added.)

Instructions:
1) Open the Fabric_ImportMode template file in PBI Desktop
2) When prompted, enter the lakehouse server and database name
3) Refresh the data.
4) Save the report with your desired name.
5) Publish the report to your desired workspace. (Can be PBI Publish or Git Push/Deploy)

OPTIONAL:
Schedule the repot and notebook to refresh at your desired schedule.  Must ensure that permissions are configured.
Note, the notebook and report do NOT have to refresh on the SAME schedule,but the report should refresh AFTER the notebook does.
You could also schedule a Fabric Pipeline to do this.
